    Real time traffic flow.

    What We Do
    Nobody wants to go eat at a dining hall only to find that there are no places to sit. With Flux real-time data reporting, you can now avoid crowded dining halls and find the best places and times to eat on campus.

    Browse.
    Browse.
    Filter dining halls by location or crowdedness.
